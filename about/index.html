About BeiLuo

Your Trusted Partner for Electronic Components with Over 20 Years of Excellence

Who We Are

BeiLuo is a leading electronic components distributor headquartered in China, specializing in providing high-quality semiconductor solutions to customers worldwide. Since our establishment, we have been committed to delivering genuine products, competitive pricing, and exceptional technical support.

As an authorized distributor for renowned brands such as Infineon, Semikron, STMicroelectronics, and many others, we offer a comprehensive portfolio of electronic components including IGBT modules, MOSFETs, MCUs, sensors, gate drivers, and power management ICs.

Our extensive inventory, combined with our deep technical expertise, enables us to serve diverse industries including automotive, industrial automation, renewable energy, consumer electronics, and telecommunications.
